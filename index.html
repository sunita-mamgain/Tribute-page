Here's some information about Mary Kom

  • Mary Kom is an indian boxer who was born in '1st march 1983'
  • She was born in Kangathei, Churachandpur, Manipur.
  • In 2000, she began training with M. Narjit Singh (Manipur State Boxing Coach) at Khuman Lampak, Imphal.
  • Kom has won the World Boxing championship five times in a row. She is the only woman to get a medal and trophy in all six world.
  • She is also the only Indian woman to qualify for boxing in the 2012 London Olympics.
